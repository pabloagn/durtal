## Overview
The resting position of book posters on cards needs a subtle enhancement so they stand out better against the dark background. Currently they blend in too much. Add a subtle shadow/glow effect to make the poster "pop" without overdoing it.

Reference: the Krasznahorkai screenshot shows how the poster sits flat against the dark card background without sufficient separation.

## Implementation Details

- Keep the effect understated -- gothic-minimal, not flashy
- Consider a very faint border or a soft `box-shadow` with low spread
- Ensure the effect works across different cover colors (light and dark)
- Do NOT overdo this -- the change should be subtle but noticeable
- Test on both grid and list views if applicable
